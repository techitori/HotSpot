PHOTO SHOOTS OF HOUSTON

Our group aims to connect those interested in photography with workshops for models, make-up artists, clothing designers and photographers all of whom that participate within the group have an awesome range of artistic backgrounds.

Whether you are just beginning or an advanced photographer, join us to discover photography at all levels in a variety of dynamic opportunities (in studio or on-location). We are working to make the group a great place for photographers, make-up artists, designers and models to start/continue to build professional portfolios. In addition, we feel this is a good place for models to meet make-up artists, designers and photographers serious about their work and that all respective parties receive useful pictures for their portfolio.

  • The Seeing, Not the Taking with Dion McInnis

    Anyone can take a photograph -- push a button. What sets each photographer apart is their vision -- what they see.

    Learn to see with all your senses and use multiple sources of inspiration to change from taking photos to seeing and creating photos. Understanding the opportunities for decisions that affect the creative outcome, photographers can listen to themselves about what inspires them, and then make the best decisions to create an image that conveys what they see with their heart and soul.

  • Galveston Coastline Workshop

    Learn to use your camera more fully in the field with illustrative in-camera techniques as we explore our favorite photographic locations on Georgia’s ST. SIMONS and JEKYLL ISLAND, and Historic BRUNSWICK too, where history’s tales and natural beauty blur the boundary between past and present.
