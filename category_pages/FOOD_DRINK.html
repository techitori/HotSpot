HOUSTON CRAFT BEER SOCIETY

If you are a craft beer enthusiast, this group is dedicated to you! Whether you are a craft beer pro, or someone who has recently developed love for beer, you are welcome. The purpose of this group is to meet other local beer drinkers, try new beers and new places, and to HAVE FUN in a casual atmosphere.

Events will revolve around meeting others who like to drink, talk about and share their love of beer. With so many great bars and festivals in Houston, the possibilities of meeting up are endless. Plus, special events will connect members with brewmasters and those in the industry for special tastings and tours.
