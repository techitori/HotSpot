Houston Area Trails & More

Houston Area Trails & More is a collection of like minded hiking enthusiast who join together for long day hikes (generally ~10 miles or more). All are welcome to join. We are not a club or organized group. There is no cost or donations asked to join the group or hike with us. Our leaders are fellow enthusiasts who volunteer by putting together a hike plan as a service for the group.

Expect the same risks and take the same precautions you would take if you were hiking alone, including the fitness level make long hikes. The organizers are not guides nor do we have any special training. We are here to come up with hiking ideas and get the hike started.

  • Camping At LCRA Lake Bastrop North Shore Park

    4 sites have been reserved; 71,72,73 & 74. These are water/electric sites. Showers and restrooms are nearby. The Maximum amount of people per site is 8, but the park may be flexible. It will be necessary to keep it to 1 or 2 person tents.

    CAMPSITE COSTS: $200.00 total for the 4 sites. Costs will be divided evenly among the campers, so if 20 people show up it will be $10.00 per person for the trip plus your park entrance fee.

    EXCESS VEHICLES: An excess fee of 10.00 will be charged by the park for all vehicles over the limit of 2 per site. The park office will let you know on the vehicle count per site at check-in. After that each vehicle will be responsible for their fee.
