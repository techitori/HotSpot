Drink & Draw Houston

Drink & Draw Houston is a live figure model social dedicated to providing a space for artists to exercise their skills with the gold standard of drawing the human figure. Network, drink, draw, meditate, and socialize with fellow Houston artists.

• NO TEACHING OR INSTRUCTION we provide a casual space to meet local creatives and refresh your skills

• NO NEED TO BE A PRO stick figures and hand-turkey’s welcome! Bring friends or a date and enjoy an evening of creative exploration!
